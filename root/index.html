What Is Stabl ?

Stabl, An App interacting with its circuit to reduce electric bills. All you have to do is set up your Monthly Electricity Bill Target. Stabl will do the rest! It'll send you a Push Notification when You are nearing your target, So you can manage how your Electricity is being used.

Easy To Use, Easy To Save

How It Works ?

Connect Stabl circuit to your MCB.

Download Stabl app and connect to WI-FI

Setup your target and save money.

How To Control

Stabl Reminds You, Whenever You Exceed Your Spending Limits. You'll Get Notifications On Stabl's App. Simply Swith Off Some Eletric Appliances, And Stabl Will Automatically Keep You In The Range Of Your Target. How Much One Saves From Stabl, Depends On his/her Own Commitment.
