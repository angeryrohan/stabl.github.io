What Is Stabl?


Stabl, An App interacting with its circuit to reduce electric bills. All you have to do is set up your Monthly Electricity Bill Target. Stabl will do the rest! It'll send you a Push Notification when You are nearing your target, So you can manage how your Electricity is being used.



Features

Know Where You Spend

Stabl tells you all about your electricity usage, with just one tap on its App.
You can know, where all is your electricity being used and how much

Reduce Electric Bills

Stabl Reminds You, Whenever You Exceed Your Spending Limits. You'll Get Notifications On Stabl's App.

Reach Your Target

Tell Stabl, What Electric Bill Amount You Want To Target, Stabl Now Sends Notifications Accordingly.
